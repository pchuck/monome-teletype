SMOOTH FLOW GRID - CROW+GRID
GITHUB/PCHUCK/MONOME-TELETYPE

IN
1: CLOCK
GRID FADERS: OCT, RANGE, DECAY
PARAM: INFLUENCES PROBABILITY

OUT
TR 1-4: /1, /2, /4, /8 CLK (R)
CV 1-4: V/OCT (GENERATIVE)
TR 5-8: CLK (DETERMINISTIC)
CV   5: ARPEGGIO
CV 6-8: MODULATIONS
CROW OUT 1-4: ENVELOPES

NOTES
J IS RANDOM OCTAVE MULT
K IS DECAY (BASED ON FADER)
X IS ATTACK (BASED ON FADER)
Z IS CHORD (0-12)
A/B/C/D IS CLK FOR DECAY
O IS FOR ARPEGGIOS
T IS CLK PERIOD
R IS RANDOM, DEGREE OF CHORD
DRUNK INFLUENCES ATTACK

TIPS
FADER 1/5/9/13 - BASE OCTAVE
FADER 2/6/10/14 - OCTAVE RANGE
FADER 3/7/11/15 - DECAY 
FADER 17 - GLOBAL ATTACK


#1
L 2 8: $ I
PROB * PRM DRUNK: BREAK
P.N 0; J * RND G.FDR.N 2 12
P.INS 0 N.C J Z R; CV $ P 0
TR.P $; CROW.AR $ X A V 5
L 0 14: G.LED I 0 / P I 512

#2
EV 2: BREAK
PROB * PRM DRUNK: BREAK
P.N 1; J * RND G.FDR.N 6 12
P.INS 0 N.C J Z R; CV $ P 0

L 0 14: G.LED I 1 / P I 512

#3

P.INS 0 N.C J Z R; CV $ P 0

L 0 14: G.LED I 2 / P I 512

#4
SKIP 8: BREAK
PROB * PRM DRUNK: BREAK
P.N 3; J * RND G.FDR.N 14 12
P.INS 0 N.C J Z R; CV $ P 0
TR.P $; CROW.AR $ * X 8 D V 5
L 0 14: G.LED I 3 / P I 512

#5
T / TIME 4; TIME 0
A * + * G.FDR.N 3 T 10 1
CV.OFF 1 * N G.FDR.N 1 12
CV.OFF 5 * N G.FDR.N 1 12
CV $ N.C 0 Z O; TR.P $

#6
B * + * G.FDR.N 7 T 10 2
SKIP 2: BREAK
CV.OFF 2 * N G.FDR.N 5 12
CV $ RND V 1; TR.P $

#7
C * + * G.FDR.N 11 T 10 4
SKIP 4: BREAK
CV.OFF 3 * N G.FDR.N 9 12
CV $ RND V 2; TR.P $

#8
D * + * G.FDR.N 15 T 10 8
SKIP 8: BREAK
CV.OFF 4 * N G.FDR.N 13 12
CV $ RND V 5; TR.P $

#M
X + / * G.FDR.N 17 T 4 1
L 1 4: G.FDR.N * I 4 5

#I
DRUNK.MIN 1; DRUNK.MAX 3
M 100; PARAM.SCALE 0 100
G.GFX 0 1 0 4 1 4 5 6 0 16 1
G.GFX 1 17 15 0 1 4 5 6 0 1 1
O.MAX 15; R.MAX 3
G.GFDR.N 0 1
